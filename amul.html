About Amul

Amul, the Taste of India, is a leading dairy brand known for its high-quality milk and milk products. Founded in 1946, Amul spearheaded India's White Revolution, transforming the country into the world's largest milk producer. Amul is owned by millions of farmers and stands as a symbol of self-reliance and quality. Amul is India's largest dairy brand.
It known for its high-quality milk, butter, cheese,ice cream, and more. It represents the strength of Indian farmers and the cooperative movement.
